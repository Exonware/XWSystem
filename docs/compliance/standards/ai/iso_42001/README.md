# ISO/IEC 42001:2023 � AI Management System Requirements

**Applicability:** Organisational governance of AI systems (v2 onwards).  
**Goal:** Implement a management system for responsible AI that integrates with existing ISMS/QMS structures.

---

## Key Elements

- Leadership commitment and policy.  
- Planning (risk/opportunity analysis, objectives).  
- Support (resources, competence, communication, documented information).  
- Operation (AI lifecycle controls).  
- Performance evaluation (monitoring, audits, management review).  
- Improvement (nonconformity, corrective actions).

---

## Planned Activities

- Draft AI management policy (aligned with GUIDE_COMP).  
- Define process interfaces with ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO 9001 systems.  
- Schedule internal audits once processes mature.  
- Maintain evidence in `docs/compliance/evidence/ai/iso_42001/`.

Status: Planning stage; to be enacted during v2 compliance cycle.
